Haysville Unified School District 261 Pre-K Breakfast Other  N/A FREEMAN ELEMENTARY

The Haysville Board of Education approved a Universal Free Breakfast program for the

2024-25 School year. The program provides all USD261 students, regardless of their family's

financial situation, with the option to eat breakfast each day school is in session for free. Families do

not have to complete any paperwork for their children to participate in the free breakfast program.
