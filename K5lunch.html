Haysville Unified School District 261 Elementary Lunch HHFKA  K-5 (age 5-10) FREEMAN ELEMENTARY

The National School Lunch Program Guidelines require we offer each student 5 food components--

* MEAT/MEAT ALTERNATE (PROTEIN)      * GRAINS      * VEGETABLES     * FRUITS         * MILK

Students are allowed to decline 2 of the 5 required food components, but grades K-8

must select at least 1/2 cup of either a fruit or vegetable.

We encourage every student to take all the foods that are offered.
